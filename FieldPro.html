Help: Deals

The Deals tab connects CapStone to your active Zoho CRM deal. Every report, asset save, and WorkDrive upload uses the deal you select here.

  • Tap Refresh from Zoho before field work so the list is current.
  • Search or filter to find the correct account, deal, or stage.
  • Select a deal card to make it the active visit.
  • Confirm the header shows the right account and deal before moving to Capture or Assets.
Deal Selection Workflow
  1. Refresh Zoho deals before field work.
  2. Search or filter by account, deal, or stage.
  3. Select the active deal before Capture or Assets.
  4. Confirm the header shows the correct account and deal.

Help: Capture

Capture is where you document the site visit: GPS, notes, photos, video, and optional report sections. CapStone saves your work locally to History first, so poor cell service does not have to cost you a full visit.

  • Confirm the active deal, technician, and GPS before documenting.
  • Tap Save Locally to History anytime — especially before generating or when signal is weak.
  • Add photos, voice notes, and only the report sections that apply.
  • Review the field checklist, then tap Generate AI Report.
  • After generating, open Report to review and save to Zoho when signal is better.

Help: Photos

Photos become part of the report, WorkDrive upload, and PDF. CapStone also saves a copy to your phone Downloads folder as a backup.

  • Video Snap Photo shots are saved to the phone automatically.
  • Gallery photos also get a CapStone copy in Downloads when phone backup is on.
  • Use Save All Photos to Phone or Save to Phone on any photo anytime.
  • Labels appear in stable WorkDrive and phone filenames.
  • CapStone warns when many photos may fill browser storage — use phone backup and export older History.

Help: Report Sections

These nine fields are optional structured notes. Fill in only what applies to this visit. Empty sections are left out of the generated report.

  • Dictate with Wispr, then tap → AI on each field to polish the text.
  • On weak or no signal, AI work queues as Pending AI and retries automatically.
  • Voice notes still work if you prefer free-form dictation.
  • Sections are saved in the Capture draft and History.

Help: Assets

The Assets tab saves equipment to Zoho Equipments, links the asset to the active Deal, and records update notes on both records.

  • Tap Pick deal & add new to choose a deal without leaving Assets (same search as Deals tab).
  • Use Request addition when AI finds a Brand or Type not in Zoho — Brad gets an email to add it to the picklist.
  • Required fields must be complete before save. CapStone highlights missing items in light red.
  • Use Save Another Asset to document multiple instruments in one visit.

Help: Search & Update

Search first whenever the instrument may already exist in Zoho. Loading a match fills the form for update instead of creating a duplicate record.

  • Search by CAC ID, serial, model, brand, type, series, name, building, or designator.
  • Matching serial + account triggers an update confirmation before save.
  • Use Replace Instrument when documenting a swap to a new unit.

Help: Nameplate Photos & AI Extract

Take or upload nameplate photos, then optionally let AI read model, serial, brand, and other visible details into the form.

  • Review every extracted value before saving.
  • Add a short photo description when prompted; it is used in the Zoho attachment filename.
  • Photo attachment failures are queued in Pending Sync without blocking the asset save.

Help: Report

The Report tab shows the AI-generated field service report after you generate it on Capture. Review everything here before saving to Zoho.

  • Check the save checklist for deal, technician, GPS, photos, and assets.
  • Save Report to Zoho updates the existing Deal note for this report instead of creating duplicates.
  • CapStone also attaches the PDF to the Deal and uploads files to WorkDrive.
  • Use Retry Report Save or Retry File Sync if any step fails.

Help: Inbox

Inbox holds unassigned voice — Plaud recordings, phone calls, or manual notes — until you link them to a Zoho deal and file a field note.

  • Capture remains for intentional walkthroughs when the deal is already selected.
  • Select the active deal on Inbox (same search as Deals) — new notes auto-link when a deal is selected.
  • Connect Plaud in Settings for auto-pull, or upload audio / add a manual note.
  • Link to deal → generate summary → save to Zoho (same pipeline as reports).
  • See docs/PLAUD_INTEGRATION.md for the full Plaud Note Pro plan.

Help: History

History stores reports on this device so you can continue work later without starting over.

  • Open + Continue restores Capture fields, photos, notes, and sections.
  • View opens the generated report only.
  • Status chips show Zoho save, PDF attachment, technician, and pending sync.
  • Continuing a saved report updates the same Zoho note when you save again.

Help: Accounts Map

The Map tab plots Zoho CRM accounts and one pin per deal on an OpenStreetMap view. Pin color reflects deal stage; inactive accounts appear gray.

  • First open defaults to Active only accounts; tap ↺ Refresh for all accounts + full geocoding.
  • Location priority: Main Site Coordinates field first, then Google Maps extension lat/lng, then address geocoding in the background.
  • Map data is cached on this device for 24 hours; tap Refresh to force a Zoho sync.
  • Filter by pipeline, deal stage, and account status. Select one or more deal stages — all Zoho stages load from your CRM; tap All stages to clear.
  • Use Pin grouping to control clustering — choose Every pin when zoomed out on Minnesota, or More groups for a cleaner overview.
  • Purple diamond pins show upcoming Zoho meetings on Active deals (linked to deal or account).
  • When several deals or meetings share a site, zoomed out they collapse into one numbered site pin — tap the pin to open the deal list right below the map and zoom in. All multi-item sites spider out to individual pins when zoomed in (busy sites also show a count on the hub).
  • Sites with 5+ items still open the scrollable list panel on tap — use the list or individual pins.
  • Tap Hide legend to collapse the deal-stage key on the map; your choice is remembered on this device.
  • Tap a pin to select the deal in CapStone (opens Capture) or open the account in Zoho CRM.
  • Accounts without a resolvable location appear in the missing-address panel.

Help: Settings

Settings controls technician identity, sync recovery, save behavior, storage, troubleshooting, and your Anthropic API key.

  • Select the technician using CapStone on this device.

  • Export History before clearing storage on a shared device.
