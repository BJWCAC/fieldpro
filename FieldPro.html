Technician Workflow Checklist
  1. Select your technician name when CapStone opens.
  2. Refresh Zoho deals and confirm the active deal before documenting work.
  3. Capture GPS, field notes, photos, and any needed structured report fields.
  4. Generate and review the service report before saving to Zoho.
  5. Confirm the Deal has the report note, attached report PDF, and WorkDrive links.
  6. For Assets, save/update the asset and confirm update notes appear on both the Asset and Deal.
  7. Use History to continue or regenerate the same report instead of starting over.
